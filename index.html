Moon Landings Were Fake
In 1969, NASA sent their rocket, Apollo 11, into space with three men on it in hopes of reaching the moon. Apollo was said to have landed at it's destination with evidence to prove it, but there is a big debate as to whether NASA actually reached the moon, or if it was just a hoax to be the first on the moon.
